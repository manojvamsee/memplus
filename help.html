Memplus Watch Interface

Welcome to Memplus.

This is a game that enhances your brain function while keeping it fun. The best part is that you get to play the full length game on a tiny watch screen. You don't even have to take out your phone.

Interaction Style

The game has two types of interactions. One way is to tap on the screen as you play the game. Other way is to choose from multiple choice questions. Every game ends and prompts you to replay when you choose a wrong option and lose the game.
